  Core Research Team Member | Xinjiang Institute of Education Project (No. XJJY202421) (2025–2026). This project examines the spatiotemporal evolution, regional disparities, spatial correlation, and influencing factors of the integration between the digital economy and the cultural-tourism industry in China. Based on complex adaptive systems theory, the study constructs an evaluation index system for measuring digital economy–cultural tourism integration and applies quantitative methods including coupling coordination analysis, Dagum Gini decomposition, spatial autocorrelation, and factor detection. My work focused on contributing to the construction of the analytical framework, modeling regional disparities and driving factors, interpreting spatiotemporal patterns, and supporting the preparation of research outputs and policy recommendations. The project aims to provide evidence for promoting digital transformation, cultural-tourism integration, and coordinated regional development.
