Research Team Member | Xinjiang Institute of Education Project (No. XJJY202421) (2025–2026). This project examines the spatiotemporal evolution, regional disparities, spatial correlation, and influencing factors of digital economy–cultural tourism integration in China. Based on complex adaptive systems theory, it constructs an evaluation framework and applies quantitative methods including coupling coordination analysis, Dagum Gini decomposition, spatial autocorrelation, and factor detection. My work contributed to framework design, modelling, pattern interpretation, and the preparation of research outputs and policy recommendations for promoting digital transformation, cultural-tourism integration, and coordinated regional development.
